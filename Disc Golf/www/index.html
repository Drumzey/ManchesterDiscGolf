    How to play

    Firstly select which tee position you are going to play from; Red tee for beginners and Blue for experienced players.

    Start from the tee position for each hole and throw towards the basket.

    Take turns to throw and always wait until everyone has thrown before heading to your disc to play.

    Your subsequent throws are from just behind where your disc lands - the player furthest from from the basket throws first.

    To complete a hole your disc must land in the basket - on top or a bounce off the chains doesn't count.

    If your disc crosses the outer boundary fence (out of bounds), or it's unplayable (in a bush!) then retrieve your disc, take your throw from where the disc crossed the boundary or within 5 metres (no nearer the basket) of where it lay in the bush, and add 1 penalty shot to your score.

    HAVE FUN!
